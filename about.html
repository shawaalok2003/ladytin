About LadyTin Weddings

At LadyTin Weddings, we bring to life elegant, unforgettable celebrations that combine rich heritage with modern luxury. Every wedding is a new chapter, and we ensure it's crafted with love and precision to reflect your unique story.

Our Story

Founded by Tina Bharadwaj, LadyTin Weddings was born out of a desire to blend timeless traditions with contemporary style. With over a decade of experience in the fashion industry, Tina brings her expertise in styling, luxury, and garment design to create weddings that are both elegant and personal.

From intimate gatherings to grand celebrations, our team works with you every step of the way, ensuring each detail is perfect, from couture ensembles to lavish decor. Our mission is to make your wedding a reflection of your love story, infused with beauty, grace, and luxury.
